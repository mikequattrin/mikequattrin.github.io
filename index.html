About Me

I am a Toronto-based Front-End Web Developer who builds powerful web applications and beautiful user experiences at SapientNitro. I love bringing stories to life by combining creative design and modern web technologies to create exciting and innovative solutions. I am passionate about delivering excellent work to help great brands tell their story.

I have had the absolute pleasure of working with a variety of amazing Canadian and international clients within the airline, retail, automotive and banking industries. In my 2+ years as a Front-End Developer, I have been involved with rolling out two large scale eCommerce platforms, a website redesign, and a few microsites. Needless to say, I like to keep busy.

When I am not writing code you can find me mountain biking, kayaking, reading, and fishing. I enjoy cooking, trying exotic foods, and brewing beer. I live to travel and enjoy taking photos and videos of the world around me.
